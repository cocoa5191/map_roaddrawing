too foriegn for home

Anyone who has left a familiar setting to establish a new one knows that 'home' is not a given place, but a construct. It is something we must build ourselves through habits, scents, and rituals.

I once felt a profound sense of homelessness, adrift between worlds. Yet, I found solace in the stories of others who shared this displacement. Knowing I was not alone gave me the strength to define home on my own terms, and I hope this space offers you the same comfort.

Input the 'materials' that define your home. Your text will be processed into an floor plan. This is a map of an indestructible home, built solely from our memories.
